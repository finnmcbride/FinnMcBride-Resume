Finn McBride

Musician for Hire

Beginning from a celebration of his parent's tastes as a child, Finn McBride pursues to homage those who paved the steps of modern music through directly-inspired covers of their songs. Beginning on the saxophone, McBride expanded his interests to a primary base in acoustic guitar and vocals.

Described as a "Jeff Buckley Shill" and "wanna be grunge," Finn will continue to stun others by his malnourished and tired looks and attempt to deal with his baggage by screaming 70% in key to songs he should never touch. But it's fun!

Many notable acts include the three piece Naked Brothers band adjacent Brain Explosion, as well as the experimental pop-synth-post-goth-gaze-black-depressive-suicidal-kpop duo Wings in Vermont, in which he beats on shit with a semblance of rhythm.
